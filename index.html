1. Introduction

Falcons Organization ("Company," "we," "us," "our," or "Developer") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how our mobile application (the "App") collects, uses, and protects your personal information. This Privacy Policy applies to the Falcons Organization mobile application available on the Google Play Store and Apple App Store (collectively, the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. Your use of the App constitutes your acceptance of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our privacy practices, please do not use the App.

2. Data Collection and What Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you use the App, you may voluntarily provide us with the following information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, password, profile picture, and other account registration details
  • Business Information: Company name, industry, job title, business size, and professional details
  • Service Request Data: Information about services you inquire about, project specifications, preferences, and budget information
  • Communication Data: Messages, feedback, support requests, and correspondence within the App
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details (processed securely through third-party payment processors; we do not directly store credit card numbers)
  • Content You Create: Any content, documents, or files you upload or create within the App

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, we automatically collect certain information:

  • Device Information: Device type, device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, mobile network information, and device settings
  • App Usage Data: Features accessed, frequency and duration of use, actions taken, pages or screens viewed, time spent on each screen, and navigation patterns
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address (collected automatically); precise location only if you grant permission
  • Crash Reports and Performance Data: Information about app crashes, errors, performance issues, and technical diagnostics
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated usage patterns, feature adoption rates, user flow analysis, and performance metrics
  • IP Address and Network Information: Your IP address, mobile carrier information, network connection type, and network performance data

2.3 Information from Third-Party Sources

We may receive information about you from:

  • Third-Party Authentication Services: If you sign in using social media accounts (Google, Apple, Facebook)
  • Business Partners and Integrations: Information shared through API integrations, connected services, or partner platforms
  • Public Sources: Information from publicly available business directories or professional networks (if you grant permission)

2.4 Sensitive Personal Information

Important: We only request these permissions when necessary for the advertised functionality. You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings.

The App may request access to sensitive information only when necessary for specific features:

  • Location Services: Precise location (only if you explicitly grant permission)
  • Camera and Photo Library: Access to take photos or upload images (only if you grant permission)
  • Contacts: Access to your device contacts (only if you explicitly grant permission)
  • Calendar: Access to your device calendar (only if you grant permission)
  • Microphone: Access for voice communication or recording features (only if you explicitly grant permission)

2.5 Data You Do NOT Need to Provide

  • NO Credit Card Storage: We do not store complete credit card numbers
  • NO SMS/Call Access: We do not access SMS messages, call logs, or call history
  • NO Biometric Data: We do not collect fingerprint or facial recognition data
  • NO Health Data: We do not collect health, fitness, or medical information

  • Encryption in Transit: SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Encryption at Rest: AES-256 encryption for sensitive data
  • Secure Authentication: Strong password requirements, optional 2FA
  • Access Controls: Role-based access restrictions
  • Network Security: Firewalls, intrusion detection, DDoS protection
  • Regular Security Audits: Penetration testing and vulnerability assessments
  • Employee Training: Regular staff training on data protection

6.2 Limitations and Risk Acknowledgment

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information. You acknowledge the inherent risks of internet communication and data transmission.

6.3 Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach, we will:

  1. Notify you without unreasonable delay (no later than 30 days)
  2. Notify relevant authorities as required (within 72 hours for GDPR)
  3. Provide details about the breach and remediation measures
  4. Offer credit monitoring or identity theft protection if applicable
